Rules of the game

1. Roll 🎲-Get points! Keep rolling to build up your turn score.

2. Hold 📥 – Save your points! This adds them to your total and passes the turn.

3. Watch Out! ⚠️ – If you roll a *1*, you lose all points from this turn and your turn ends.

4. To Win 🏆 – Be the first to reach *100 total points*! (Player 1 starts, then Player 2).

5. Reset 🔄 – Press "New Game" to start over.
